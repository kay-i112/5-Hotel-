A Vision for Liberia

A Message from
Our Founder

"I founded Grand Grunder with a singular conviction: that Liberia deserved a hotel the whole world would travel to — not just pass through. Our land has beauty, warmth, and culture that rival any destination on earth, and my mission was to build a stage worthy of it."

Bruce Wenneh is a Liberian entrepreneur, philanthropist, and hospitality visionary whose career spans infrastructure development, tourism investment, and community empowerment across West Africa. Grand Grunder Hotel is his landmark achievement — a property that has redefined five-star standards on the continent and created over 400 direct jobs for Liberians.

"We do not simply offer rooms. We offer Liberia — her people, her spirit, her future."

— Bruce Wenneh
