A Vision for Liberia

From the Founder's Desk

"I founded Grand Grunder with a singular conviction: that Liberia deserved a hotel the whole world would travel to — not just pass through."

Bruce Wenneh is a Liberian entrepreneur, philanthropist, and hospitality visionary whose career spans infrastructure development, tourism investment, and community empowerment across West Africa.

Grand Grunder Hotel is his landmark achievement — a property that has redefined five-star standards on the continent and created over 400 direct jobs for Liberians, rooted in the SKD Sports Complex Community on ELWA Road.

— Bruce Wenneh
